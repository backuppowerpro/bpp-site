Why not a standby generator?

A standby whole-home generator is automatic, it's wonderful, and it's overkill if you just want the lights, fridge, WiFi, and well pump through a storm. You've already got the portable generator. The inlet + interlock puts it to work through your panel, sized to what you actually need.

FAQ, Greer homeowners

Does my generator need a 240V outlet? Yes, either a 30A (four-prong twist lock) or 50A (four-prong). If yours is 120V-only, it's not compatible with this install. Upgrade first, then book.

Do I need permission from Duke Energy? No. The interlock mechanically prevents backfeeding so the install is code-compliant and doesn't require utility approval.
