Privacy Policy

Backup Power Pro, a service of Key Electric, LLC Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Backup Power Pro, a division of Key Electric, LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website, submit a quote request, download our generator guide, or engage with our services. We respect your privacy and are straightforward about what we collect and why.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Give Us Directly

When you fill out our quote form or download our generator guide, we collect:

  • Name, to address you and prepare your quote
  • Phone number, to confirm your appointment and send follow-up texts
  • Email address, to send your quote, confirmation, and any follow-up communication
  • Home address, to confirm service area eligibility and prepare for installation
  • Information about your electrical panel and generator, amperage, panel type, and installation details shared during the quote process

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, certain data is collected automatically through cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Pages visited and time on site, used to understand how visitors use our site
  • Device type, browser, and operating system, used for site performance and compatibility
  • Referring URL and traffic source, used to understand where our visitors come from (e.g., Facebook, Google, direct)
  • IP address, collected by analytics and pixel tools; not stored or used by us directly

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To contact you about your quote or appointment, by phone, text, and email
  • To prepare and send your quote, through our secure client management system
  • To schedule and complete your installation, we need your address and contact info to do the job
  • To send confirmation and reminder messages, automated texts and emails around your scheduled appointment
  • To send the generator guide, if you requested it, we deliver it and may follow up with related tips
  • To measure and improve our advertising, we use the Meta Pixel and conversion tracking to understand which ads lead to bookings so we can show better ads
  • To request a Google review, after your installation is complete, we may send a review request by text

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your name, phone number, email, or address with any third party for their own marketing purposes.

3. Text Message (SMS) Communications

When you request a quote on our website, you opt in to text messages in one of two ways, depending on the form: (1) by checking an SMS consent box that is not pre-checked and is optional, or (2) by tapping the quote button under an on-screen disclosure that says you agree to get texts about your quote and install. Either way, the opt-in is affirmative and specific to Backup Power Pro (Key Electric LLC). These messages may include:

  • Your quote details and pricing
  • Scheduling confirmations and installation reminders
  • Installation updates and follow-up communications

Consent is not a condition of purchase. You may request a quote and receive service without consenting to SMS messages by contacting us directly at key@backuppowerpro.com.

Text messages are sent by Backup Power Pro via Twilio. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to opt out. Reply HELP for assistance.

We do not share or sell mobile opt-in data or SMS consent information with any third party for marketing or promotional purposes. Your phone number is used solely to communicate with you about your quote and installation.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies, small files stored in your browser, to support analytics and advertising measurement. The Meta Pixel and Microsoft Clarity place cookies when you visit the site. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling them may affect some site functionality.

We do not use cookies to track your activity across other websites beyond what Meta and Clarity handle as described above.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as needed to provide our services and meet legal obligations. Customer records (quotes, invoices, job details) are retained for a minimum of five (5) years for tax and contractor license compliance purposes. Marketing contact records are retained until you opt out or request deletion.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Opt out of texts: Reply STOP to any text message from us at any time.
  • Opt out of emails: Use the unsubscribe link in any email we send, or contact us directly.
  • Request your data: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information. We will honor this request unless retention is required by law (e.g., tax or licensing records).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at key@backuppowerpro.com or call (864) 863-7800.

8. California Residents, CCPA Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: You can request details about the personal information we collect, use, and share.
  • Right to Delete: You can request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale: We do not sell your personal information to third parties. You do not need to take any action to opt out.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

We do not sell your personal information, to data brokers, advertisers, or anyone else. Full stop.

To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at key@backuppowerpro.com. We will respond within 45 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for homeowners aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Payment processing is handled entirely through Stripe and their payment partners, we do not store credit card numbers or banking information on our systems. Our website is served over HTTPS. Third-party tools we use (Stripe, Meta, Twilio) maintain their own security standards.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we do our best to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website or services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. The current version is always available at backuppowerpro.com/privacy.
