The quick fix

Extension Cords

Cheap, but risky
  • Cannot power hardwired circuits like your furnace, well pump, or lights
  • Fire and carbon-monoxide risk
  • Not code-compliant, and you set it up from scratch every outage
Recommended

Generator Inlet + Interlock

One all-in price
  • Safe, permanent, permitted, and inspected
  • Powers your essential circuits through your panel
  • Works with the generator you already own, done in one day
The big-ticket option

Whole-Home Standby

A major investment
  • Fully automatic, but a major expense
  • Often a months-long waitlist
  • More system than a portable-generator backup plan calls for
